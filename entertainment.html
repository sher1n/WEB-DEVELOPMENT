Elemental is continuing to show major growth at the worldwide box office. This puts it ahead of last year's Pixar flop Lightyear ($226.4 million worldwide) and places it on a trajectory to surpass Disney's Encanto ($256.8 million worldwide) to become Disney's biggest animated hit since the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic.
